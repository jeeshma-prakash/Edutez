COURSE OVERVIEW

Course Overview

Our Stock Marketing course is designed to provide you with a thorough understanding of the stock market. From basic concepts to advanced strategies, this course covers everything you need to know to navigate and succeed in the stock market. Gain practical skills in market analysis, investment planning, and risk management, and start building a portfolio that aligns with your financial goals.

Understanding Stock Markets

Learn the basics of stock markets, including how they operate, the types of stocks, and key market players.

Technical Analysis

Master the techniques of analyzing stock price movements using charts and indicators to make informed trading decisions.

Fundamental Analysis

Explore how to evaluate a company's financial health and growth potential through financial statements and market conditions.

Investment Strategies & Risk Management

Develop effective investment strategies and learn how to manage risk to protect and grow your investments.
CURRICULUM OVERVIEW

Comprehensive Stock Marketing Curriculum

Our curriculum provides a detailed exploration of stock marketing, from foundational principles to advanced investment techniques. Through hands-on exercises and real-world scenarios, you’ll gain the skills needed to analyze markets, develop strategies, and make informed investment choices.

  • Introduction to Stock Market
  • Types of Markets
  • Market Valuation, Face Value, Market Value, and Dividends

  • Market Trends and Analysis
  • Basics of Technical Analysis
  • Price Charts, Japanese Candlesticks, Support & Resistance, Price Patterns

  • Trading Types and Market Terms
  • Technical Patterns (Single and Multi-Candlestick Patterns)
  • Trading Tools (Moving Averages, MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands)
  • Fibonacci Retracement

  • Investing Strategies, Investor Types, and Market Analysis
  • Risk Management (Stop Loss, Position Sizing)
  • Mutual Funds and ETFs
  • Q&A, Live Analysis, and Major Project Presentation
