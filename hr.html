COURSE OVERVIEW

Course Overview

Our Human Resource course provides an in-depth exploration of HR principles and practices. From recruitment and talent management to employee relations and compliance, this course covers all aspects of HR to help you build a successful career in human resources. Gain practical skills in managing HR functions, addressing workplace challenges, and contributing to organizational growth.

Talent Acquisition & Recruitment

Learn effective strategies for attracting and hiring the right talent to meet organizational needs and drive performance.

Employee Development & Training

Discover how to design and implement training programs that enhance employee skills and support career growth.

Performance Management

Explore techniques for setting performance goals, evaluating employee performance, and providing constructive feedback.

HR Compliance & Legal Issues

Understand the legal aspects of HR management, including compliance with labor laws and regulations to ensure a fair and ethical workplace.
CURRICULUM OVERVIEW

Comprehensive HR Curriculum

Our curriculum offers a detailed examination of human resource management, focusing on both strategic and operational aspects. Through real-world scenarios and practical exercises, you’ll develop the skills needed to effectively manage HR functions and contribute to organizational success.

  • Basic Definition of Training
  • Boosting Performance, Productivity, Skills, and Knowledge

  • HR Planning, Recruitment, Performance Management, Career Planning, Rewards, and Industrial Relations

  • Job Analysis, Job Descriptions
  • Compensation Models: Flat Rate, Merit Pay, Profit and Gain Sharing

  • Perspectives of Training and Development
  • Training Needs and Core Competencies
